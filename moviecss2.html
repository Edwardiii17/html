The Lion King

This movie is a Disney animated movie about a lion cub named Simba that loves exploring! His dad Mufasa always kept Simba safe when he got into trouble. Simba has a cruel uncle named scar that wanted to rule the throne and take Mufasa's place. He lured Simba and Mufasa into a stampede of wildabeests but Simba survives but Mufasa is killed. Simba returns to the kingdom as and adult to take back his land from his uncle Scar with his new best friends Pumbaa and Timon.

Main Actors

  • Jonathan Taylor Thomas (young Simba)
  • Matthew Broderick (grown Simba)
  • James Earl Jones (Mufasa)
  • Jeremy Irons (Scar)
  • Ernie Sabella (Pumbaa)
  • Nathan Lane (Timon)
