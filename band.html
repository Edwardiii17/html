Top 3 Albums

  • Tha Carter IV

Album released on August 29th, 2011 by Cash Money Records. The album won a Grammy Award for Best Rap Album and a Billboard Music Award for Top Rap Album.

  • Tha Carter III

Album released on June 10th, 2008 by Cash Money Records. The album won a Grammy Award for Best Rap Album and also a BET Hip Hop Award for CD of the Year.

Album Released on March 22nd in 2013 by Young Money Entertainment. The album won the World Music Award for World's Best Album.
